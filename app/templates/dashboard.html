Executive Summary

This report presents the findings of a geo-spatial AI analysis of 239,368 fire incidents recorded by the Jordanian Civil Defense Directorate between 2018 and 2025. The analysis integrates historical fire GPS coordinates, 8-year climate data from the Open-Meteo API, and K-Means Machine Learning to produce an optimized resource allocation plan.

def fire_risk_alert_pipeline(region: str): forecast = open_meteo_api.get_7day_forecast(region) score = compute_risk_score(forecast.max_temp, forecast.total_rain, forecast.wind) if score > 65: hotspots = kmeans_model.get_hotspots(region=region) for zone in hotspots: sms_api.broadcast(zone.residents, message=ALERT_TEMPLATE) civil_defense_api.increase_patrol_frequency(region, factor=2)
