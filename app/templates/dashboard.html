Executive Summary

This report presents the findings of a geo-spatial AI analysis of 239,368 fire incidents recorded by the Jordanian Civil Defense Directorate between 2018 and 2025. The analysis integrates historical fire GPS coordinates, 8-year climate data from the Open-Meteo API, and K-Means Machine Learning to produce an optimized resource allocation plan.

How the Model Works
We fit a Degree-2 Polynomial Regression to 8 years of regional fire counts (2018–2025). The model captures non-linear acceleration patterns — curves and plateaus that simple linear regression would miss. Projections assume no major policy interventions and reflect historical climate and land-use trends.
